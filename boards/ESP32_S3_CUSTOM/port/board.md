The following firmware is applicable to the ESP32-S3-Custom board based on ESP32-S3.

This board must be equipped with 16 MiB external SPI Flash and Octal PSRAM (8MB). The default configuration enables the ESP32-S3 target, native TinyUSB CDC/MSC, UART REPL, the ESP-VISION 16 MiB flash layout, GC2145 DVP camera support, SPI LCD (320x240) support, ES8311 audio codec, and FT5x06 touch controller.
