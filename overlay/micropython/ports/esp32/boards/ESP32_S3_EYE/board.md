The following firmware is applicable to the ESP32-S3-EYE development board
based on ESP32-S3.

This board must be equipped with 8 MiB external SPI Flash and 8 MiB PSRAM. The
default configuration enables the ESP32-S3 target, native TinyUSB CDC/MSC, UART
REPL, the ESP-VISION 8 MiB flash layout, OV2640 camera support, ST7789 LCD
support, and 1-bit SD card support.
