The following firmware is applicable to the ESP32-S31-Korvo development board
based on ESP32-S31.

This board must be equipped with 16 MiB external SPI Flash and Octal PSRAM. The
default configuration enables the ESP32-S31 target, native TinyUSB CDC/MSC, UART
REPL, the ESP-VISION 16 MiB flash layout, OV3660 DVP camera support, RGB LCD
support, and 4-bit SD card support.
